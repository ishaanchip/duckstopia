The Task

Your job is to solve to the riddle. Throughout this mission, there will be a variety of questions in which you must provide the correct answer. If the question is answered correctly, you will recieve one part of the riddle. Make sure to keep track of all the riddle fragments you collect and after the questions you will put the pieces together. After you solve the riddle, you will recieve a mystery item...

To help you on your journey, Charlie will offer his assitance whenever you feel neccesary. Click on him when a question appears to recieve a hint!
